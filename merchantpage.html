Our Story

Founded in 2008, Cebu Artisan Co. began as a small family workshop in the heart of Cebu. Our mission is to bridge the gap between ancient pottery traditions and modern Filipino homes.

Every piece you see is thrown on a wheel by a master artisan, fired in high-temperature kilns, and hand-glazed using eco-friendly materials sourced locally from the Visayas region.

"We don't just make pots; we mold the earth of our ancestors into the heritage of our children."
